About Me

Hello! I’m Dipanshu Malik, a passionate and driven Computer Science Engineering student from Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak, graduating in June 2025. I specialize in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, and Full Stack Web Development.

I have a strong command over Python, Java, C++, SQL, Flask, React.js, and JavaScript, along with experience working with AI frameworks like Hugging Face, LangChain, and Google Gemini API. My technical expertise is complemented by my curiosity and commitment to continuous learning.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on several exciting projects:

  • WIZORA AI: A feature-rich browser extension enabling document chat, webpage Q&A, CSV analysis, AI chatbot, and image generation.
  • Smart AI: An intelligent application to interact with PDFs and websites for Q&A using advanced AI models.
  • Heart Disease Prediction: A machine learning model with 89% accuracy predicting heart disease risk.
  • Price Prediction Model: A forecasting tool using machine learning algorithms for price prediction.
  • Personal Portfolio Website: A responsive portfolio to showcase my work and journey.
I also completed a Data Analytics and Visualization Simulation with Accenture, where I analyzed multiple datasets, uncovered insights, and presented data-driven recommendations for a social media client.

Beyond coding, I’m a strong believer in collaboration, problem-solving, and continuous growth. I enjoy taking on challenges that push me beyond my limits and help me develop solutions that create real value.

I’m always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and innovative ideas. Let’s connect and build something impactful together!
